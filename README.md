# ☕ Monday Coffee Business Analytics | SQL Market Expansion Case Study

> A business-focused SQL analytics project that evaluates sales performance, customer behavior, and market potential across multiple Indian cities to support strategic expansion decisions for a fictional coffee retail brand.

![Banner](https://github.com/parrithi/Monday-Coffee-Business-Analytics/blob/main/Monday___Coffee.png)

---

# 📖 Executive Summary

Every successful retail business relies on data-driven decisions before expanding into new markets.

In this project, I analyzed transactional sales data from **Monday Coffee**, a fictional coffee chain operating across multiple cities in India. Using PostgreSQL, I explored customer purchasing behavior, revenue trends, product demand, and market potential to identify cities with the highest growth opportunities.

The project concludes with actionable recommendations for business expansion based on customer demand, revenue generation, rental costs, and estimated coffee consumption.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

The management team wanted answers to several strategic questions before opening new coffee outlets.

Key business objectives included:

- Which cities generate the highest revenue?
- Where is customer demand strongest?
- Which products perform best in each city?
- How fast are sales growing over time?
- Which cities offer the best balance between revenue and operating costs?
- Where should the company prioritize its next expansion?

# 📂 Dataset Overview

The analysis uses four relational tables:

- **City** – Population, estimated rent, city rankings
- **Customers** – Customer demographics and locations
- **Products** – Coffee product catalog
- **Sales** – Transaction-level sales data

Together, these tables simulate a real-world retail database used for business reporting.

# 📈 Key Business Insights

The analysis identified several high-potential markets for business expansion.

### 🥇 Pune
- Highest overall revenue
- Strong average customer spending
- Low rent per customer
- Best overall profitability

### 🥈 Delhi
- Largest estimated coffee consumer base
- High customer acquisition potential
- Suitable for long-term market expansion

### 🥉 Jaipur
- Highest number of active customers
- Lowest rent per customer
- Excellent opportunity for cost-efficient growth

---

# 💡 Strategic Recommendation

Based on revenue, customer demand, rental efficiency, and market potential:

### Immediate Priority

✅ Pune

Offers the strongest combination of profitability, customer value, and operational efficiency.

### Growth Market

✅ Delhi

Ideal for scaling due to its massive consumer base and high market potential.

### Cost-Efficient Expansion

✅ Jaipur

Low operational costs combined with a strong customer base make Jaipur an attractive emerging market.
